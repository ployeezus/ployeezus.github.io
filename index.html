ABOUT ME

Aspiring engineer, programmer, and photographer

I'm currently a senior in highschool. I was born in Thailand and moved to U.S. when I was 6. I was always interested in science and design when I was little. During my sophomore year of highschool, I joined my school's robotics team. We've won a lot of banners and awards. I currently run their website, but I also take pictures and do A/V for IndianaFIRST. During my junior year of highschool, my AP computer science teacher convinced me to apply to the Catapult program at Nextech. This program has shown me really cool tech opportunities all around Indianapolis. In the future, I would love to major in mechanical or electrical engineering and minor in computer science.
